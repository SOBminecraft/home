Rules of Shades Of Brown SMP
(The things which we expect everyone to follow):



  1. DO NOT use Creative Mode.

  2. No changing of Gamerules apart from the ones collectively agreed by all players.

  3. No Building in "Spawn Chunks" + 2.

  4. Get all wood(logs) from trees, no floating bushes.

  5. Irrespective of who kills the dragon first, Dragon Egg belongs to every player on server.

  6. No building in Central End Island, apart from something with the combine agreement between all players.

  7. No claiming or killing of villagers leveled up by other players.

  8. No random blocks where they don't belong, apart from Bridging or Navigational.

  9. Clean up your messes.

  10. No building of farms built by other players, apart from the ones that come under common category.

  11. No griefing. Please do not destroy, deface, graffiti, or build on top of another person’s constructions.

  12. Don't break down walls in peoples property to get in.(Does not apply to their personal mines, but does to something like underground houses)

  13. No 1×1 tall towers or holes.

  14. Maximum of 30 of each animals per person.

  15. No modifying others circuits.

  16. You can't kill on neutral ground.

  17. Don't take any item not clearly marked as free or public (Unless if provided permission to do so) even from builds that looked abandoned.

  18. Don't kill other players, their pets/animals, or any name tagged mob. Unless specifically stated otherwise by the other player that owns the pets/animals, or the name tagged mob, or that they want to engage in PvP combat.

  19. Do not build to closely to other people creations or claim land too close to their claim. (Unless specifically given permission to do so by the owner of the build/claim).

  20. No reckless use of Spectator Gamemode.

  21. No selling of items in the shop more the prices mentioned on this Website

  22. Villagers in the shopping district are banned due to raids possibly starting there.
